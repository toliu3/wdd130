Activity

Mr. Liu Wanxing, the first ancestor in Taiwan, was born on March 22 of the lunar calendar. The Liu surname clan gathers and commemorates the ancestors on the Saturday before March 22 in the lunar calendar. The commemoration ceremony starts at 10:00 in the morning, and the clan will gather together in the evening for a meal.

Liu Wanlai collected the family tree and compiled a family tree of the Liu surname, which was published in 1993. All genealogical information has been stored on the FamilySearch website, and interested people can visit the website for inquiries.
